Reserve Your Table

An Oasis of Sophistication

Perched above the azure waters of Santorini, our bar and lounge offers an unparalleled experience of luxury and relaxation. With a carefully curated selection of premium spirits, rare wines, and handcrafted cocktails, we invite you to unwind in style.

Our mixologists combine local ingredients with international flair to create signature drinks that capture the essence of the Mediterranean. Whether you're enjoying a sunset aperitif or a moonlit nightcap, every moment at Azurea is designed to delight.

Open daily from 4pm until late, with live music on weekends and special tasting events throughout the season.
