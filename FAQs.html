Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about your stay at Azure Santorini Palace

Your Questions Answered

What types of accommodations does Azure Santorini Palace offer?

Azure Santorini Palace offers a range of luxurious accommodations:

  • Royal Suites with private infinity pools
  • Honeymoon Villas with private jacuzzis
  • Executive Sea View suites
  • Family Penthouses with multiple bedrooms
  • Deluxe Garden Rooms with private patios

All accommodations feature premium amenities, luxury linens, and stunning views of either the Aegean Sea or our lush gardens.

What time is check-in and check-out?

Our standard check-in time is 3:00 PM and check-out is 11:00 AM.

Early check-in and late check-out may be available upon request, subject to availability and possibly an additional charge. Please contact our concierge team in advance to arrange this.

What dining options are available at the palace?

Azure Santorini Palace features several exceptional dining venues:

  • Azure Restaurant: Our signature fine dining experience with sunset views
  • The Terrace: Casual all-day dining by the pool
  • Vine Cellar: Premium wine bar with local Santorini vintages
  • In-Room Dining: 24-hour gourmet room service

We accommodate all dietary requirements with advance notice. Our chefs specialize in Mediterranean cuisine with locally-sourced ingredients.

Is breakfast included in my stay?

Breakfast is included for all guests staying in our suites and villas. Our lavish breakfast buffet is served daily from 7:00 AM to 11:00 AM at Azure Restaurant, featuring:

  • Fresh local fruits and juices
  • Artisanal breads and pastries
  • Greek yogurt with honey and nuts
  • Eggs and omelets made to order
  • Selection of cheeses and cold cuts

Guests in our Garden Rooms may add breakfast for €35 per person per day.

What amenities and services does the palace offer?

Our palace offers world-class amenities including:

  • Infinity pool with private cabanas
  • Full-service luxury spa and wellness center
  • 24-hour concierge and butler service
  • Private beach access with complimentary loungers
  • State-of-the-art fitness center
  • Complimentary WiFi throughout the property
  • Valet parking and Tesla charging stations

Additional services like private yacht charters, helicopter transfers, and guided tours can be arranged by our concierge.

What is your cancellation policy?

Our standard cancellation policy varies by rate plan:

  • Flexible Rate: Free cancellation up to 7 days prior to arrival
  • Advance Purchase Rate: Non-refundable, but may be modified for a fee
  • Peak Season: 14-day cancellation policy applies June-August

We strongly recommend travel insurance for unexpected changes. For specific booking conditions, please refer to your confirmation email or contact our reservations team.

Do you offer airport transfers?

Yes, we offer premium private transfer services from Santorini Airport (JTR) and Athinios Port:

  • Luxury Sedan: €85 one-way (max 3 guests)
  • Premium SUV: €110 one-way (max 5 guests)
  • VIP Minivan: €150 one-way (max 8 guests)

Transfers include meet-and-greet service and bottled water. Helicopter transfers can also be arranged. Please book transfers at least 48 hours in advance through our concierge team.

Is Azure Santorini Palace family-friendly?

Absolutely! While we cater to romantic getaways, we also welcome families with children aged 6 and above. Our Family Penthouse is specially designed for families, featuring:

  • Two separate bedrooms
  • Child-friendly amenities upon request
  • Special children's menu in all restaurants
  • Kids' pool area (open seasonally)
  • Babysitting services (advance notice required)

Please note our Royal Suites and Honeymoon Villas are adults-only (18+) for the comfort of all guests.

Can you accommodate special requests or celebrations?

We specialize in creating unforgettable experiences and would be delighted to arrange:

  • Anniversary and honeymoon packages
  • Birthday surprises and celebrations
  • Private dining experiences (beachfront, rooftop, or in-suite)
  • Romantic turndown services
  • Custom excursion itineraries
  • Spa packages and in-room treatments

Please contact our concierge team at least 7 days prior to arrival to discuss your special requests. For weddings and large events, we recommend contacting us at least 6 months in advance.

Still have questions?

Our concierge team is available 24/7 to assist with any inquiries about your stay at Azure Santorini Palace.
