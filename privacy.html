Azure Santorini Palace - Terms & Conditions

Last Updated: June 15, 2023

1. Reservations

All reservations at Azure Santorini Palace are subject to availability and these terms. To guarantee a reservation, we require:

  • A valid credit card
  • Complete guest information including full name, contact details, and arrival time
  • Acceptance of these terms and conditions

2. Cancellation Policy

Cancellation policies vary by rate plan and season:

  • Standard Rate: Free cancellation up to 7 days prior to arrival
  • Non-Refundable Rate: Full prepayment required, no cancellations or changes permitted
  • Peak Season (June-August): 14-day cancellation policy applies

No-shows will be charged the full amount of the reservation.

3. Payment

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express). A pre-authorization may be required upon check-in for incidentals. All prices are in Euros (€) and include VAT at the current Greek rate.

4. Guest Responsibilities

Guests agree to:

  • Respect all palace property and other guests
  • Comply with all safety and security regulations
  • Be responsible for any damages caused during their stay
  • Not exceed the maximum occupancy of their accommodation

5. Check-In/Check-Out

Check-in time is 3:00 PM and check-out is 11:00 AM. Early check-in and late check-out may be available upon request for an additional fee.

6. Children Policy

Children under 12 stay free when sharing existing bedding with parents. Additional charges apply for extra beds. Some accommodations are adults-only (18+).

7. Pets

Small pets (under 15kg) are allowed in designated accommodations with prior approval, subject to a €50 per night cleaning fee.

8. Liability

Azure Santorini Palace is not responsible for loss or damage to guest property, or for injuries sustained on the premises except where caused by our negligence.

9. Governing Law

These terms are governed by Greek law. Any disputes shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the Greek courts.

Azure Santorini Palace - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 15, 2023

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information to provide and improve our services:

  • Contact Information: Name, email, phone number, address
  • Booking Details: Stay dates, room preferences, special requests
  • Payment Information: Credit card details (processed securely)
  • Usage Data: Website interactions, preferences, feedback

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information helps us:

  • Process and manage your reservation
  • Provide personalized service during your stay
  • Improve our services and guest experience
  • Send marketing communications (with your consent)
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Data Sharing

We may share your information with:

  • Service providers essential to your stay (e.g., payment processors)
  • Legal authorities when required by law
  • Third parties with your explicit consent

We never sell guest data to third parties.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • SSL encryption for online transactions
  • Secure storage systems
  • Limited access to personal data
  • Regular security audits

5. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for:

  • Providing services you requested
  • Complying with legal obligations (typically 7 years for financial records)
  • Resolving disputes

6. Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict processing
  • Withdraw consent for marketing
  • Lodge a complaint with the Hellenic Data Protection Authority

7. Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses cookies to:

  • Enable essential functionality
  • Analyze site usage (Google Analytics)
  • Personalize your experience

You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Significant changes will be communicated to guests via email or on our website.

9. Contact Us

For privacy-related inquiries or requests, please contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@azuresantorini.com or +30 22860 21234.
