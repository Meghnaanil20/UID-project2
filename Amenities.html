Resort Amenities & Services

Experience unparalleled luxury with our world-class facilities designed to make your stay unforgettable.

Our Amenities

At Azure Santorini Palace, we've curated every detail to ensure your comfort and enjoyment. From rejuvenating spa treatments to exhilarating water sports, discover the perfect balance of relaxation and adventure.

Infinity Pools

Three stunning infinity pools with breathtaking views of the Aegean Sea, including an adults-only pool and a family-friendly option with a shallow area for children.

Azure Spa

Our award-winning spa offers traditional Greek therapies, massages using local olive oil, and bespoke treatments in private cabanas overlooking the sea.

Beachfront Dining

Four distinct dining venues including our Michelin-starred seafood restaurant, a casual beach grill, a wine bar with 200+ Greek wines, and 24-hour room service.

Fitness & Wellness

State-of-the-art gym with personal trainers, sunrise yoga sessions on the beach, Pilates studio, and nutrition counseling to maintain your wellness routine.

Water Sports Center

Complimentary paddleboards, kayaks, and snorkel gear. Also available: jet ski rentals, scuba diving excursions, and private yacht charters.

Azure Kids Club

Daily activities for children ages 4-12 including Greek mythology workshops, sandcastle competitions, and movie nights with babysitting services available.

Explore Santorini's Wonders

Beyond our resort, discover the magic of Santorini with volcanic boat tours, sunset cruises, wine tasting at ancient vineyards, and visits to iconic blue-domed villages.
